Our Love Story

First time we meet

Nov 12,2021

We met for the first time at Marlow Park, nervously walking along the river, trying to act cool. The day was gorgeous, and despite the butterflies, we couldn't help but feel something special was starting.

First Getaway

Dec 25,2021

Our first holiday together was in St. Vincent, where we swapped nerves for sun, sea, and Caribben Cruise coctails! Between beach days and adventures, we discovered that traveling together felt just as easy as being home.

Marriage Proposal

Nov 12,2021

I proposed to Karissa on the pier of our hotel, right as she was getting out of the sea, glowing from the sunset. I was too nervous to get down on one knee—didn't want the ring falling into the ocean! So, I sat her down, shared some heartfelt words, and asked her to spend forever with me.

Our Engagement

Being engaged has given me a whole new level of confidence—like I can take on the world! But let’s be honest, it also comes with a bit of pressure. After all, Karissa is someone I want to spoil and provide for…without losing my mind in the process!
